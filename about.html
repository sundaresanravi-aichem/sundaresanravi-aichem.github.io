About Me

I am a recent Chemistry (Honours Track) graduate from SRM Institute of Science and Technology. My training revolves around applied computational chemistry and machine learning, employing methods like density functional theory (DFT) using VASP and Gaussian, alongside Python-based data-driven workflows.

My primary motivation is tackling the scalability and efficiency limitations of classical electronic-structure methods. I am actively developing foundational knowledge in data-driven and automated approaches to chemistry, aiming to master AI-driven workflows for predicting chemical reactivity and accelerating molecular innovation.
